The fragrance is often called "Bcarat" or "Bacarat Fragrance" describing the scent inspired by Baccarat Rouge 540, one of the iconic perfumes from Maison Francis Kurkdjian. This fragrance is stunning with elegant floral notes, such as rose and jasmin, combined with a fresh touch of berries, giving an alluring feminine impression. The addition of cinnamon provides a luxuriously warm touch, while vanilla and amber add softness and alluring appeal. The deep foundation of the scent is enhanced by musk, creating a complex and inviting harmony for an unforgettable fragrance experience.
